How to Install Your Email Signature

  • Gmail: Go to Settings → See all settings → General → Signature → Paste your copied signature → Save.
  • Outlook Desktop: File → Options → Mail → Signatures → New → Paste your signature → Save.
  • Apple Mail: Mail → Preferences → Signatures → Select account → Paste your signature.
